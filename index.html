The Problem

Freight Trains Are Blocking Your Roads

Across hundreds of U.S. cities, freight trains stop on roads without warning. Commuters wait. Ambulances reroute. School buses stall. And no one knows when it will clear.

In a survey of 100+ residents in Muncie, Indiana, reported wait times ranged from 5 minutes to over an hour — with no way to check before driving into the delay.
